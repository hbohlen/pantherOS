# Swarm Research Command

## Overview

The `/swarm-research` command orchestrates parallel research efforts across multiple domains, coordinating specialized research agents to investigate complex topics and synthesize findings into actionable insights.

## Usage

### Syntax
```bash
/swarm-research <research_plan>
```

### Parameters
- `<research_plan>`: Detailed description of research objectives, scope, and requirements

### Examples

#### Basic Research
```bash
/swarm-research Investigate best practices for NixOS module development
```

#### Complex Multi-Domain Research
```bash
/swarm-research Analyze AI memory layer architecture including:
1. Container orchestration patterns with Podman
2. Graph database optimization with FalkorDB
3. Knowledge graph design with Graphiti
4. Integration patterns with opencode.ai SDK
5. Performance optimization strategies
6. Security considerations for persistent memory
```

#### Gap Analysis Research
```bash
/swarm-research Identify gaps in current pantherOS documentation and specifications:
- Missing module documentation
- Incomplete security specifications
- Test coverage gaps
- Process documentation needs
- Knowledge base deficiencies
```

## Research Process

### Phase 1: Research Planning
1. **Requirements Analysis**: Parse research objectives and scope
2. **Task Breakdown**: Decompose research into parallelizable tasks
3. **Resource Allocation**: Assign specialized researchers
4. **Timeline Planning**: Establish research schedule and milestones

### Phase 2: Swarm Coordination
1. **Task Distribution**: Assign research tasks to specialized agents
2. **Progress Monitoring**: Track research progress and dependencies
3. **Bottleneck Resolution**: Address research blockers and issues
4. **Quality Assurance**: Ensure research quality and completeness

### Phase 3: Knowledge Synthesis
1. **Findings Aggregation**: Collect results from all research streams
2. **Pattern Analysis**: Identify patterns and correlations
3. **Conflict Resolution**: Resolve contradictions in findings
4. **Insight Generation**: Generate actionable insights and recommendations

### Phase 4: Reporting
1. **Executive Summary**: High-level findings and recommendations
2. **Detailed Findings**: Comprehensive research results
3. **Action Plans**: Specific next steps and implementation guidance
4. **Confidence Assessment**: Quality and reliability metrics

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### Research Scope Creep
**Problem**: Research expanding beyond original scope
**Solution**: Re-focus on core objectives, defer secondary research

#### Coordination Delays
**Problem**: Research tasks not completing on schedule
**Solution**: Reallocate resources, adjust timeline, prioritize critical paths

#### Conflicting Findings
**Problem**: Contradictory research results
**Solution**: Investigate sources, validate methodologies, seek consensus

#### Low Confidence Results
**Problem**: Research findings with low reliability
**Solution**: Additional research, source validation, expert consultation

### Resolution Strategies

#### Quality Assurance
1. **Source Validation**: Verify source reliability
2. **Methodology Review**: Ensure sound research methods
3. **Peer Review**: Validate findings with multiple researchers
4. **Evidence Checking**: Verify supporting evidence

#### Timeline Management
1. **Milestone Tracking**: Monitor progress against plan
2. **Resource Reallocation**: Adjust resources as needed
3. **Scope Adjustment**: Modify scope to meet timeline
4. **Prioritization**: Focus on critical research items
